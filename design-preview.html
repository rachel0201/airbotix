08 · voice & tone

Confident, not boastful. Warm, not childish.

✓ DO
  • "Teach kids to code with AI."
  • "An early-stage pilot, focused on doing it right."
  • "100+ kids, 2 schools, real curriculum."
  • "Built with educators, in real Australian schools."
  • "You're a parent who wants more for your kid."
✗ DON'T
  • "Transformative AI literacy paradigm."
  • "Industry-leading K-12 EdTech innovator."
  • "5,000+ students globally" (if not real)
  • "Empower the next generation of digital natives."
  • "Unlock your child's full potential."
